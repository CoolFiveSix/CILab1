Short Biography

I am a Ph. D. student of Computer Software and Theory at the Big Data Analysis Group, State Key Laboratory of Software Development Environment, Beihang University, under Prof. Yongxin Tong and Prof. Ke Xu‘s supervision. Previously, I obtained a B.Eng. with a major in Computer Science and Technology, from Beihang University.

My research interests broadly span the intersection of Algorithms, Big Data, Security/Privacy and Mechanism Design. I am interested in both foundations and applications of problems that broadly fall in these domains. I am especially interested in research that balances effectiveness and efficiency.

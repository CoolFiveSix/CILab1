Biography

I am currently a post-doctoral researcher at the Big Data Analysis Group, State Key Laboratory of Software Development Environment, Beihang University. I received my Ph.D. degree in computer science and technology from Beihang University in 2022, under the supervision of Prof. Yongxin Tong and Prof. Ke Xu. Previously, I receive a B.Eng. degree with a major in computer science and technology from Beihang University in 2017.

Reasearch Interest

My research focuses on the intersection of Algorithms, Big Data and Security/Privacy. I am interested in both foundations and applications of problems that fall in these domains. My recent research topics are:

  • Security & Privacy
  • Spatial Databases
  • Federated Learning
  • Online Algorithm
